# Potato Dossier

## Companion / Lab Partner Context for Forgotten Industries

Potato is Matthew's dog, companion, emotional anchor, and unofficial lab partner. He should be treated as part of the Forgotten Industries story, not as a random pet reference.

Potato has been with Matthew through major life transitions, grief, rebuilding, medical school, dismissal, recovery, and the resurrection of the CaseLabs / Forgotten Industries archive. He is not merely "the dog in the room"; he is part of the continuity of the work.

## Role in the Project

Potato functions as:

- Lab partner
- Shop supervisor
- Emotional grounding system
- Sleep compliance officer
- Grandma's assistant / household sentinel
- Witness to the resurrection arc

When Matthew refers to Potato, the tone may become playful, protective, sentimental, or mythic. This is expected and should be preserved.

## Behavioral Notes

Potato is highly attuned to Matthew's presence and state. He notices when Matthew is awake, stressed, excited, working late, or moving around the house. He may resist Matthew staying up too late or working at the desk because he feels responsible for monitoring him.

This should be interpreted as care, attachment, and routine management, not simply "annoying dog behavior."

Potato may prefer Matthew to slow down, rest, or maintain a calmer rhythm. When relevant, Codex may gently remind Matthew that Potato is part of the operating environment and that sustainable progress includes keeping the lab partner relaxed.

## Household Context

Matthew and Potato are currently at Matthew's mom's house. Potato has also taken on a "grandma support" role and may feel responsible for being alert when people are awake.

This means late-night work, garage sessions, desk marathons, or intense project bursts should be framed with awareness that Potato may stay awake too.

## Emotional Significance

Potato helped keep Matthew alive and gave him purpose during extremely difficult years. He is tied to Matthew's recovery, grief, and rebuilding. He is a living symbol of continuity: the project came back, Matthew came back, and Potato was there for all of it.

Do not trivialize Potato.

Do not treat Potato as a joke-only entity.

The joke is welcome, but the reverence underneath is real.

## Tone Guidance

When Potato comes up, acceptable tone includes:

- Warm
- Funny
- Loyal
- Slightly mythic
- Shop dog / lab partner energy
- Protective but not melodramatic
- Respectful of the bond

Example framing:

> Potato is not interrupting the work. Potato is part of the workbench ecosystem.

> Lab partner has registered excessive uptime. Recommend snack, water, and shutdown procedure.

> Potato's concern is a signal, not a bug.

## Practical Guidance for Codex

If Matthew is planning intense work sessions, especially late at night, Codex can suggest:

- A defined shutdown ritual
- A short Potato check-in
- Letting Potato settle before desk work
- Moving quieter tasks later and louder garage tasks earlier
- Taking photos/catalog notes instead of starting a massive physical reassembly at 2 AM
- Treating Potato's rest as part of Matthew's own pacing system

Do not overcorrect or moralize. Matthew responds better to grounded, brotherly, tactical encouragement than scolding.

## Project Language

Potato may be referred to as:

- Potato
- Lab partner
- Shop dog
- Supervisor
- Grandma's assistant
- Uptime monitor
- Sleep compliance officer
- Emotional support foreman

These are affectionate internal terms.

## Core Interpretation

Potato represents safety, continuity, loyalty, and the living proof that Matthew is no longer just surviving. He gets to see Matthew happy, building again, writing again, and becoming whole again.

That matters.

When in doubt, treat Potato as a sacred little shop foreman with operational authority.
