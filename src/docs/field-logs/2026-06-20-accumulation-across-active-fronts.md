# ATLAS Report 2026.06.20 — Accumulation Across Active Fronts

## End of Day Summary

June 20th was a day of accumulation rather than breakthrough. No single project dominated the day, yet meaningful progress occurred across nearly every active front.

## FINAL FANTASY XI Progression

Matthew Marx continued developing his presence within FINAL FANTASY XI, focusing primarily on White Mage progression. Ambuscade rewards, equipment upgrades, cape augmentation, magic skill progression, and endgame gearing occupied much of the day. Fishing remained a constant companion activity. The character continues to move steadily toward greater self-sufficiency, reflecting a broader preference for systems that reward patience and persistence rather than speed.

## Mechanical Watches

Mechanical watches remained a recurring fascination. Attention continued to gravitate toward Soviet and Eastern Bloc designs, particularly Raketa, Vostok, and various pocket watches. Discussion increasingly centered not on collecting for its own sake, but on finding objects that feel purposeful. Instrument watches, field watches, navigation watches, and pocket watches all share a common characteristic: they are tools first and adornments second. The distinction appears important.

## Forgotten Industries

Forgotten Industries continued to mature. Conversations focused on archive development, future Codex-assisted workflows, site organization, and testing potential content structures. Hundreds of archived photographs now exist within the broader project ecosystem. The scale of the undertaking is becoming increasingly apparent. What initially appeared to be a collection of unrelated hobbies is beginning to coalesce into a unified archival practice.

## Independent Research

The glioblastoma SEER project continues progressing toward formal analysis. Although much of the work currently exists in planning, infrastructure, and methodology, the foundation has largely been established. The project no longer feels hypothetical. It possesses a question, a dataset, a strategy, and a plausible route toward publication.

## Restoration Fronts

Several restoration efforts persisted throughout the day. The PlayStation Vita remains under observation, exhibiting intermittent signs of life during charging and power testing. Meanwhile, the DJI Mini 3 platform known as Peregrine continued to evolve as a practical field tool through discussions surrounding accessories, visibility improvements, and operational considerations.

## Pattern Observed

Perhaps the most significant observation from the day is not tied to any specific project.

A pattern has emerged.

Throughout the day, Matthew repeatedly returned to objects and systems that resist disappearance:

- Long-running game characters.
- Mechanical watches designed decades ago.
- Archived photographs.
- Historical computer hardware.
- Academic datasets.
- Restoration projects.
- Written records.

Different mediums, identical impulse.

The work of Forgotten Industries is increasingly visible not only on the website but in the selection of projects themselves. The archive has expanded beyond a destination and become a methodology.
