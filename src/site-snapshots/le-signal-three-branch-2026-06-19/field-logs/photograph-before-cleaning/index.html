Principle

Recovery starts with the object as found. Dust, missing hardware, bad assumptions, and provisional assemblies are part of the record.

Method

Photograph the object before cleaning. Label uncertainty. Record what is visible, what is missing, and what cannot yet be known.

Finding

The technical record protects the emotional truth from performance. Evidence first. Interpretation later.
