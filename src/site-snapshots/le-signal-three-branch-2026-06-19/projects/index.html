CaseLabs Mercury S8 + Pedestal Restoration

Revived Started 2015 Restored 2026

Rebuilding a 2014 time capsule

Documentation and restoration of a white powder-coated CaseLabs Mercury S8 with pedestal, stored for roughly a decade and returned to the bench as part hardware archive, part recovery log, part proof of life.

The ship comes back in sequence.

Progression

The ship comes back in sequence.

  1. 01

    Recovery

    Chassis, pedestal, panels, cooling hardware, and related parts returned to the bench after long storage.

  2. 02

    Inventory

    Core case, pedestal, panels, radiators, pumps, Aquaero, fans, reservoir, and fittings entered into the archive record.

  3. 03

    Manual Path

    Recovered assembly reference and Manual 001 preserve the missing instruction trail.

  4. 04

    Bench Verification

    Confirm tray type, panel configuration, mount inventory, radiator condition, pump behavior, and QDC path.

  5. 05

    Proof Build

    Assemble a functional loop with available hardware before making any sale or final disposition decisions.

  6. 06

    Dossier

    Turn the restoration into a complete project record: photos, part states, decisions, test results, and provenance.
