Operator

Matthew Taylor Marx

Former one-person custom PC builder under Forgotten Industries around 2014 and 2015. Scientific and medical background. ASU Biological Sciences graduate. Former KU medical student. Interested in perfusion, technical medicine, restoration, archive practice, and hands-on systems.

Forgotten Industries is the archive: the evidence shelf, restoration notebook, project dossier system, public source record, and art-life pressure valve.

What About Art?

The question belongs in the machinery.

The archive is not only a restoration ledger. It is also music, design, jokes, taste, photography, grief, strange objects, and the life around the machines. The point is not to explain art away. The point is to leave room for it to keep speaking.

Origin

The evidence comes first.

The CaseLabs project was purchased around 2015 and stored after Matthew's father died. Returning to it in 2026 is part restoration, part inventory, part personal reconstruction. The point is not to make suffering useful. The point is to make the evidence visible.
